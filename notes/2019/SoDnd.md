# So... D&D

## So, who’d be up for D&D?

* Need to know lots of rules? No.
* Long play sessions? No.
* Invest time outside of play? No.
* Long-term commitment? No.

## Essentially

* DM says what’s going on.
* Players say what they want do.
* [Dice are often rolled here].
* DM says you what happens.

“The DM describes the locations and creatures that the adventurers face, and the players decide what they want their characters to do. Then the DM determines the results of the adventurers’ actions and narrates what they experience.”
