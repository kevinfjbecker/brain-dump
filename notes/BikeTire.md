# Bike Tire

## Stuff I need

* Drill
* Ratcheting screwdriver
* Bucket with water
* Vegetable oil
* Dish soap
* Bicycle pump
* AirPods
* Telephone
* Variable screwdriver
* Pair of spoons
* Bike keys

## Steps to follow

1. Take off the foot thing
1. Stand up the bike
1. Strap the bike up
1. Take the wheel off
1. Take the tire off
1. Take the tube off
1. Fill the bucket of water
1. Find the hole
1. Check the tire
1. Put some air in the tube
1. Replace the tube
1. Get the tire back on
1. Get the wheel back on
1. Get the foot thing back on
