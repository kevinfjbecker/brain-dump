# Logistics Game

This is a game about picking things up and putting them away.

Play proceeds in short rounds. Each player gets a chance to play each role for a few minutes.

Roles

* Expediter
* Picker
* Runner

Detail

Expediter: decides where a thing needs to be put. Things need not only to put “away”, but “where it is supposed to be.”

Picker: picks “mess” up loose, out of place items, and brings them to the expediter.

Runner: takes the thing and put it where the expert it I said to

Alternative play

All of the players take on each the roles, in phases. Each player picks up an item. The players come together and collaborate on where each item should go. Each player takes her or his item to “where it supposed to be.”
