Start with an unconnected graph of vertices

Drag from one vertex to the next to link flow of military power.

Tap a vertex to bring context menu with sliders that adjust the economic and military and scientific attributes of a planet.

The context menu includes a slider for military expenditure rate, which affects either a buildup or draining of military power.

Economic energy is spent either on military or scientific endevor, which is regulated by a slider.

Scientific energy is either spent on economic or military development, which is regulated by a slider.

Planets have an intrinsic resource value, which is consumed by military/economic/scientific activity.
